About

Coffee is a popular beverage made from beans of the Coffea plant. The main types are Arabica and Robusta, each with distinct flavors. The beans undergo processes like roasting before brewing. Coffee comes in various styles, from espresso to cold brew. It contains caffeine, providing a stimulating effect. Coffee has a rich cultural history and is a major global industry. Enjoyed for its taste and social aspects, moderation is key for its health benefits.
